****ALCHEMY SPEHRE IN-GAME REFERENCE
***KEY COMMANDS
The hjkl keys govern movement, like so:
    u k i   
   h  .  l   
    n j m   
While the . key means "stay in place".
***CHARACTER ACTIONS
, - pick up an item
. - rest
; - auto-rest (rest until recovered. Useful after level-ups)
i - inventory
q - quaff
e - eat
w - equip/weild/wear
f - fire at the closes monster
