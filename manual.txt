****ALCHEMY SPEHRE IN-GAME REFERENCE

***KEY COMMANDS
The hjkl keys govern movement, like so:
    u k i   
   h  .  l   
    n j m
While the . key means "stay in place".

The arrow keys also work, but whatever.

***CHARACTER ACTIONS
, - pick up an item
. - rest
; - auto-rest (rest until recovered. Useful after level-ups, gives monsters turns)
i - inventory
f - fire at the closest monster with your current ranged weapon

***INVENTORY
w - equip/weild/wear/eat/quaff
d - drop
t - dequip/unweild/take off
i - close inventory
